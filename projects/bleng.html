Bleng

With Bleng, you can find the recipes for the smoothie you want! You will find over 100 smoothie recipes, very easy to compose. You don't even have to make the effort to go shopping, just enter the ingredients you already have at home in the search engine and Bleng will show you all the possible smoothies available. You can then browse all the recipes you like best and decide which smoothie to make.
